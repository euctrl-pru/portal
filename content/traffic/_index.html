--- title: Traffic ---

The dashboard below provides an overview of the evolution of flights (all controlled flights including overflights) and en-route service units in the EUROCONTROL Member State Area. The analysis of the en-route service units is based on the en-route charging zones for which the Central Route Charges Office of EUROCONTROL (CRCO) collects en-route charges 1.

The results are shown in the dashboard below and also available for download in our data repository.
