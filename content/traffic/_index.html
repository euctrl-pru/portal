--- title: Traffic ---

The dashboard below provides an overview of the evolution of flights (all controlled flights including overflights) and en-route service units in the EUROCONTROL Member State Area. The analysis of the en-route service units is based on the [en-route charging zones][ert_cz] for which the Central Route Charges Office of EUROCONTROL (CRCO) collects en-route charges [^1].

The results are shown in the dashboard below and also available for download in our data repository.

Traffic Flows

Intra-ECAC traffic typically accounts for around 80% of the total network traffic (excluding overflights). The share was 79% in 2019, the last pre-pandemic year, it reached 81% in 2020 and 2021 as domestic traffic was less affected than international traffic by the COVID-19 pandemic and, as we can see below, is 80% in 2022.
