--- title: Daily traffic variation by Area Control Center (ACC) date: '2020-04-16' ---

This dashboard shows the daily traffic variation1 for the European ACC compared to 20192.
Observed variations are the result of many different factors including: COVID-19, Overall economic downturn, Failure of airlines, ANSPs’ disruptions, Airports’ disruptions, Traffic variation in 2019. * The graphics on the right present the daily evolution, the average evolution of each week (starting on Mondays) and the moving average over the last 7 days.

  1. Traffic at ACC level = Total IFR Flights controlled by each ACC.↩︎

  2. The comparison with the previous year is adjusted to fit the cosest comparable day: i.e. Monday 03/02/2020 is compared to Monday 04/02/2019.↩︎
