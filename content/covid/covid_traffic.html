--- title: COVID-19 - Impact on traffic date: '2020-04-16' ---
This dashboard shows the daily traffic variation1 for the main European Aircraft Operators compared to 20192. Observed variations are the result of many different factors including: COVID-19, Overall economic downturn, Failure of airlines, ANSPs’ disruptions, Airports’ disruptions, Traffic variation in 2019.
  • The graphics on the top present the daily evolution, the average evolution of each week (starting on Mondays) and the moving average over the last 7 days.
  • The bottom left graphic presents the breakdown of the Traffic3 variation for each ACC of the selected State.
  • The bottom right graphic presents the breakdown of the Traffic4 variation for the main airports of the selected State

State and Airline response to the COVID-19 crisis

  1. Traffic at State level = Total IFR Flights (including international arrivals, departures and domestics).↩

  2. The comparison with the previous year is adjusted to fit the cosest comparable day: i.e. Monday 03/02/2020 is compared to Monday 04/02/2019.↩

  3. Traffic at ACC level = Total IFR Flights (including international arrivals, departures, domestics and overflights).↩

  4. Traffic at Airport level = Total Departing and Arriving IFR Flights from/to each airport.↩
