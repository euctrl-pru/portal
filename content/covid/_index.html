--- title: Focus on the COVID-19 outbreak date: '2020-05-15' ---


Complementary to the EUROCONTROL web page which provides daily situation updates in an interactive dashboard, the goal of the PRC site is to provide relevant high level snapshots of the crisis, particularly related to performance topics as more data becomes available.

As a result of COVID-19, the number of flights operating daily in EUROCONTROL Member States declined in March-April by up to -90% compared to the same period in 2019. The analysis below shows the evolution of the daily flights in the EUROCONTROL Member States for the years 2010, 2019 and 2020. The year 2010 was included as a reference because of the volcanic ash crisis from 14-20 April which brought European aviation close to a standstill.
