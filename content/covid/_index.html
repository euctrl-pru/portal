--- title: Focus on the COVID-19 outbreak date: '2020-05-15' ---


As a result of COVID-19, the number of flights operating daily in the EUROCONTROL area declined in March-April by up to -90% compared to the same period in 2019.

Complementary to the EUROCONTROL web page, the goal of this page is to support the industry and interested parties with a consolidated view of relevant information on the impact of the COVID-19 crisis on the aviation industry, particularly related to performance topics.

For daily traffic statistics by State, Area Control Center (ACC), Airport, and Aircraft Operator level please visit our interactive dashboard!
