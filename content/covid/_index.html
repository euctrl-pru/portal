--- title: Focus on the COVID-19 crisis date: '2020-05-15' ---

With the outbreak of the COVID-19 pandemic in Europe in early 2020, the focus changed entirely from solving the European en-route capacity crisis to managing an unprecedented global crisis of the aviation industry affecting the entire value chain.

Complementary to the EUROCONTROL web page, the goal of this page is to support the industry and interested parties with a consolidated view of relevant information on the impact of the COVID-19 crisis on the aviation industry, particularly related to performance topics.
