Traffic variation by }}>ANSP

2019 (Jan-Sep) vs. 2018 (Jan-Sep)


Between January and September 2019, 26 of the Air Navigation Service Providers (ANSPs) included in the analysis reported a traffic increase compared to 2018. In absolute terms, ENAV, DSNA, Croatia Control, ENAIRE, HCAA (Greece) and SMATSA (Serbia and Montenegro) showed the highest year on year growth.
