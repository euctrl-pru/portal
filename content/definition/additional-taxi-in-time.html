--- title: Additional Taxi-in Time slug: additional-taxi-in-time bibliography: [../../static/bib/aviation.bib] ---

The additional taxi-in time provides an approximate measure of for the average holding time during the taxi-in phase, during times that the apron and stands are congested. This indicator is calculated as the difference between the actual taxi-in time and a reference taxi-in time estimated for each runway-stand combination.

Unit of Measurement

Minutes per IFR arrival [min/arr].
