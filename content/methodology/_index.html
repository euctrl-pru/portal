Safety

Capacity

  • ATFM delay computation
  • Airport Capacity Utilisation

Efficiency/ Environment

  • Horizontal Flight Efficiency performance indicators
  • En-route Vertical Flight Efficiency
  • Vertical Flight Efficiency during Climb and Descent
  • Unimpeded ASMA Time metric
  • Additional ASMA Time performance indicator
  • Unimpeded Taxi-out Time metric
