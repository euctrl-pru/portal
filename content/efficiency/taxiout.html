--- title: Taxi-out additional time ---

ANS-related inefficiencies on the departure flow are measured in terms of additional taxi out times.

Additional taxi-out times have a direct impact in terms of fuel burn and emissions. The higher the additional time, the higher the additional fuel consumption and emissions. The results are available for download in our data repository.

Please note that the analysis may take a few moments to load. The dashboard is best viewed in Chrome or Firefox.
