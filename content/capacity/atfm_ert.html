--- title: En route air traffic flow management (ATFM) delays date: '2020-04-30' ---

The interactive dashboard below enables an analysis of en-route ATFM delays by delay cause as attributed by the local Flow Management Position (FMP) in the EUROCONTROL area.

The underlying data is available for download in the data section.

Please note that the analysis may take a few moments to load. The dashboard is best viewed in Chrome or Firefox.
